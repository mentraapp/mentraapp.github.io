🧠 Science-Based Mental Health Tests

MenGauge brings together widely used screening questionnaires, adapted for easy self-checks

PHQ-9 (Patient Health Questionnaire)

Understand depression symptoms and severity.

GAD-7 (Generalized Anxiety Disorder)

Measure anxiety levels.

DASS-21 (Depression, Anxiety, Stress Scales)

Explore three key areas of mental well-being.

PSS (Perceived Stress Scale)

See how stressful you find life situations.

BAI (Beck Anxiety Inventory)

Identify common anxiety symptoms.

AUDIT (Alcohol Use Disorders)

Review your relationship with alcohol.

DAST-10 (Drug Abuse Screening)

Self-check for drug use problems.

MDQ (Mood Disorder Questionnaire)

Screen for possible mood disorders such as bipolar disorder.

These questionnaires are recognized by clinicians worldwide and give you reliable, research-backed insights in just a few minutes.

⚠️ Important Disclaimer

MenGauge is a self-help screening tool. It is not a medical device and does not provide a clinical diagnosis. Results should not replace professional evaluation. If your scores are in the moderate or severe range, or if you are experiencing distress, please consult with a qualified healthcare provider.


If you are in crisis or thinking about harming yourself, please seek immediate support from your local emergency number or a trusted mental health helpline. You are not alone.
